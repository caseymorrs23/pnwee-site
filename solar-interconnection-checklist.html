Washington Solar Interconnection Checklist

Use this practical checklist to prepare a clean, utility-ready interconnection package. It mirrors our internal workflow, with steps you can follow or hire us to complete under PE supervision.

Download the PDF (Email Required)

1) Site Assessment & Load Review

  • Roof/ground mount feasibility, tilt, shading
  • Service size, panel schedules, transformer margins
  • Existing EV/large loads and demand diversity

2) Preliminary One-Line Diagram

PV array(s), inverter(s), battery integration, protection devices, interconnection point, and metering.

3) Utility Pre-Application

Utility-specific forms and study thresholds (PSE, Avista, Pacific Power, SnoPUD, Seattle City Light, etc.).

4) Detailed Design & PE Stamp

Load calcs, conductor sizing, protection settings, short-circuit checks, and code compliance. We stamp and coordinate assumptions.

5) Submit to Utility & AHJ

Align documentation and forms to avoid rework or delays.

6) Construction & Inspection

Coordinate adjustments, redlines, and closeout notes to match field conditions.

7) Final Interconnection Approval

Permission to operate (PTO), meter configuration, and optional monitoring.
