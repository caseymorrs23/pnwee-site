EV Charging Load Calculations in Washington: A Practical Guide

For DCFC and Level 2 deployments in WA, we combine connected and continuous loads with realistic diversity and PF to avoid over-sizing while protecting uptime.

Key steps

  • Establish connected & continuous kW/kVA per nameplate
  • Apply demand/diversity factors appropriate to use pattern
  • Check PF assumptions when aggregating mixed loads
  • Validate feeder/panel schedules and protective device ratings
  • Coordinate with the utility on transformer capacity & margins

Deliverable: a clear calc sheet and a stamped summary suitable for submittal.
