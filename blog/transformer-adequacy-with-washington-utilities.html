Transformer Adequacy with Washington Utilities: What to Prepare

A practical checklist we use for adequacy requests across WA. The goal is to minimize back-and-forth and keep projects moving.

Utility-ready package

  • Summary memo with connected/continuous load and diversity basis
  • One-line with clear load aggregation and protective devices
  • Assumptions: PF, duty cycles, simultaneity
  • Recommended margin for near-term growth

Result: fewer questions, faster approvals.
