Do EV Charging Stations in Washington Require a P.E. Stamp?

Most commercial, multifamily, or public EV charger installs require a P.E.-stamped set of drawings for permitting in Washington.

What AHJs Expect

  • Site plan, bollards, ADA access, and conduit routes
  • One-line diagram with feeders, panels, OCPD
  • Load calcs with demand/managed charging notes
  • Manufacturer cut sheets for EVSE, breakers, disconnects
