Electrical Load Calculations for Permit Submittals

PNW Electrical Engineering prepares NEC-compliant electrical load calculations for residential, commercial, multifamily, and EV charging projects. These studies help contractors verify service capacity, support permit review, and document whether existing equipment can handle new loads.

What We Provide

  • NEC service and feeder load calculations
  • Service upgrade studies and capacity checks
  • Tenant improvement and equipment-add calculations
  • EV charger load calculations and demand review
  • Permit-ready summaries for AHJ and utility review

When Contractors Typically Need This

You usually need a load calculation when adding EV chargers, upsizing service equipment, remodeling tenant spaces, installing large HVAC or process loads, or responding to plan review comments. We provide clear calculations that help move submittals forward.

We work directly with electrical contractors, developers, and owners who need permit-ready documentation, quick turnaround, and clear communication during submittal and plan review.
